Who is Dylan Goliber?

About Dylan

Dylan graduated from Appalachian State University with a Bachelor's in Chemistry with a concentration in marketing and business. He contributed to many research projects during his time at Appalachian State, and worked as a research assistant in Dr. Petia Bobadova's research lab where he performed the computational modeling of BODIPY, a fluorophore, in its ground and excited states. He currently works as a quality control chemist at Stallergenes Greer.

Hobbies & Interests

Dylan spends his free time volunteering at his church and practicing music for their worship team.
